EPII:-
In this project ,"We thought about those people who have a first level of high glucose or those people who take always injection insulin through the injectionar in body,we created one feedback or working model where our project automatically measure the glucose levels and when glucose level high it is automatically inject insulin in body." Here is where we created the feedback for our project. We used tap water, which has low resistance and high conductivity, in place of insulin, and we used distilled water, which has high resistance and low conductivity, in place of blood. We also added a motor, which flows from tap water to distilled water because the latter has a higher voltage than the former. The motor automatically turns off when the two readings of distilled water are equal. Here is a quick overview of our project.
